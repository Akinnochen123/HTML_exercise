Let's add a recipe for some delicious pancakes

We are going to borrow a nice pancake picture from wikipedia

Here is what the recipe calls for

Ingredients

  • 2 or 3 Eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of Butter
  • 1 cup of Milk
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 cups of Flour
  • 1/2 spoon baking soda
  • 5 tablespoons of sugar

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat until creamy. Add in the dry ingredients
  2. Melt the butter in a microwave-safe bowl
  3. Add the butter and milk to the mix
  4. Heat the frying pan to a medium low flame
  5. Sprinkle a few flecks of water onto your pan
  6. Pour about 3 tablespoons to 1/4 cup batter from the tip of a large spoon or from a pitcher onto the hot griddle or greased frying pan
  7. Cook for about two minutes or until the pancake is golden
  8. Cook the other side until golden and remove
  9. Enjoy!
